TEAM UNIVERSITY - NEBBI STUDY CENTER

Welcome to Team University - Nebbi Study Center. This Center conducts distant learning under Team University, Kampala. Here is where we offer quality higher education from Diploma, Bachelors Degree to Post-graduate Diploma.

We are located in an environment of peace and tranquility, away from the noises of the town. It is just the right place for education. Our academic stafflist is composed of men and women of stature, determination and dedication to serve the community. This gives us the uniqueness in the way we offer higher education.
